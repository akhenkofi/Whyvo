Refund Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Subscription Charges

Subscription payments are generally non-refundable once access has been provisioned.

Exceptions

  • Duplicate charges
  • Billing errors caused by platform malfunction

Request Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the charge date with transaction reference.

How to Request

Contact FarmSavior support and include your account phone/email, transaction reference, and reason.
